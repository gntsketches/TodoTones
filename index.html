Todo Tones


Todo Tones is an app for algorithmic music generation. Pitches and other sound settings are controlled by the text of each todo-item. To get started, enter any of the 12 chromatic pitch-names (C, F#, Bb, etc.)

Each todo is editable by clicking. ('Enter' creates a new line, so use 'Tab' or click elsewhere to finish.) Default settings are used if they are not specified. Text entry is case-insensitive. Some formatting (such as '<>', or '{}') is applied for clarity (but you don't need to use it).
